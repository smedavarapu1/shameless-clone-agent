# Validation findings - data-accuracy warnings & refresh cadence

Source: 46-agent cross-source validation + adversarial review, 2026-06-07 (21 members verified). Re-read before re-rating any politician or trusting any reported return figure.

## Data-accuracy warnings (read before trusting any return number)

1. **Trade-date vs disclosure-date is the single biggest distortion.** Every headline (Unusual Whales, Quiver, academics) measures from the TRADE date. A copy-agent can only enter at/after the STOCK Act disclosure (up to 45 days late). Realized copy-returns are systematically LOWER and are the only investable figure. Never compare a UW/Quiver headline to what the agent can actually capture.
2. **Most headline % figures are effectively single-source (Unusual Whales).** Davidson, Norcross, Sewell, Steil, Sessions, Wyden, Chip Roy, McGovern, Stauber, Bean, Pou, Latta all trace to ONE UW report echoed by Finviz/Benzinga/Motley Fool. Apparent multi-source agreement is repetition, not independent confirmation.
3. **Whole-portfolio annual return vs purchase-mimicking/cumulative can flip the sign** on identical disclosures. UW = calendar-year active-position return; Quiver = "follow this politician" cumulative. Eggers & Hainmueller (annual) found NO outperformance; Ziobrowski (purchase-only) found large; Belmont (clean 2012-2020) found NONE. "Congress beats the market" is NOT settled.
4. **Dollar amounts are imputed from ranges.** STOCK Act discloses only brackets; trackers use the range midpoint (top tier is open-ended). Large undisclosed sizing error in every % and net-worth figure - why some trackers show absurd values like +198,000%. Treat all dollar/percent figures as order-of-magnitude.
5. **Directly contradictory windows exist - trust the contradiction, not the headline.** McGarvey +105.8% (2024) vs -15.2% (1Y); Collins +77.5% vs +9.8% (1Y); Chip Roy -59% (UW) vs -9.4% (Quiver 1Y); Norcross +70.8% vs underlying +4.36%. Swings of 50-120 points by window/method are common.
6. **Options cannot be priced from disclosures** (no strike/expiry/premium). Pelosi is the only options-driven name; her headline is options-amplified and NOT equity-replicable. Use ONLY her equity-stripped clone (~21% CAGR).
7. **Single-mega-winner distortion dominates the top of the leaderboard.** Rouzer (NVDA), Wyden (NVDA), Schultz (New Gold), Sewell (NVDA), Davidson (GE), Steil (GE Vernova), McGarvey (NVDA), Bucshon (DWAC), Crenshaw (META). Strip the one name and the "skill" evaporates.
8. **Attribution errors:** spouse/dependent/advisor-managed accounts get folded into "the member's" return. Confirmed managed/spouse: Wyden, Collins, McGarvey, Chip Roy, Pelosi (Paul Pelosi). Copying "the member" often means copying someone else's old buy-and-hold.
9. **Survivorship bias:** members who lose/retire/resign exit the dataset. Bucshon (retired), Crenshaw (lost primary), MTG (resigned Jan 2026) are frozen - backward-looking only.

## SOP recommendation (from the validation)

1. **Use ratings as a filter, not a signal source.** The cohort produces almost no actionable equity-copy signal.
2. **Only Pelosi's equity book clears the bar** - lag-adjusted, expectations capped at the equity-clone level, signal ends Jan 2027.
3. **Treat any star with <10 trades or a single-position driver as untradeable.** Require ≥10 diverse trades AND >1 return-driving position.
4. **Hard-exclude:** fully-divested / no-live-signal (McGarvey, Crenshaw, Davidson, Steil, Rouzer), retired/resigned (Bucshon, MTG), spouse/advisor-managed-no-edge (Collins, Wyden, Roy, McGarvey), fund-markdown-not-trades (Bean, Pou, McGovern).
5. **Never fade a negative return** - all single-position or holdings-snapshot artifacts.
6. **Require two independent methodologies** before trusting any magnitude (Quiver per-trade, CapitolTrades raw, or own PTR parse).

## Refresh cadence

- **Annual hard refresh** every January when the UW Congress Trading Report drops (primary source for nearly every figure). Re-rank within ~2 weeks.
- **Quarterly light refresh:** re-pull each active member's trailing trade flow (CapitolTrades / House Clerk PTRs) to catch new trades that change "no live signal" calls.
- **Event-driven refresh** on: resignation/retirement/primary loss (kills forward signal), new STOCK Act violation/late disclosure (recompute replicability), or divestment/blind-trust announcement (flip to UNRATED).

## Spotting a stale rating (re-verify if ANY apply)

1. No new disclosed trade in 6+ months for a name rated on "active trading."
2. Member retired/resigned/lost-primary since the rating date.
3. Rating's headline % predates the most recent UW annual report, OR the member moved in/out of the UW top/bottom-10 (non-persistence).
4. Rating rests on a single un-recomputed source - provisional until a second methodology confirms.
5. Driving position not cross-checked against the live House Clerk / Senate filing.

Assume a rating older than 3 months is provisional; older than 12 months (one report cycle) is stale and must be recomputed before any production weighting.
